About us

women++

The Women++ in Computing Society at City, University of London is a community dedicated to addressing the challenges faced by women in computing disciplines and the broader technology sector. Our society was established in 2023 by a group of passionate City students and academics who recognize the importance of promoting gender diversity in technology.

The team

The Women++ in Computing Society at City University of London is led by a passionate and dedicated committee comprised of both students and academic staff. Our committee members play a vital role in driving the vision and mission of our society, ensuring that we create a supportive and inclusive environment for all.

Our committee members are not only leaders within the Women++ in Computing Society but also advocates for change in the wider tech community. They strive to create a positive impact by challenging gender stereotypes, fostering a culture of inclusivity, and empowering women to excel in computing.

Together, as a united committee, we are making a difference and shaping the future of computing for women. Join us in this empowering journey and be a part of the Women++ in Computing Society committee today!
